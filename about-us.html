About Us

We SMART TECHNOLOGY (Pvt) Ltd. commenced our humble journey in 2003. Today, we have achieved many milestones in the IT industry over two decades. Our accolades and recognition by well-known multinational brands prove our position as the No.1 IT service provider in the southern province of Sri Lanka.

With 5 branches located in Galle and Matara, we cater to a wide range of products and represent leading brands such as HP, ASUS, Dell, EPSON, Canon, and HIK Vision. Our offerings include desktop computers, laptops, printers, computer accessories, office automation products, CCTV solutions, and gaming systems. We specialize in delivering comprehensive IT solutions for homes and businesses, including network setups, security solutions, and public address systems.

Who We Are

Our founder, Mr. Sameera Tharindu Bopage, began his career in IT education with a vision to make IT accessible and affordable to society. Under his leadership, SMART TECHNOLOGY has grown to provide top-notch ICT solutions, ensuring availability at every doorstep in the southern province.

Mr. Bopage's dedication to high ethical standards and business principles has built a reputable brand with satisfied customers across the region. His focus is now on expanding to Colombo and Gampaha in the near future.
